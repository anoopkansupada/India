üëî Understanding "Smart Casual" Dress Code

Since many of your dinners specify "Smart Casual," here's exactly what that means for both women and men:

üëó Smart Casual for Women

Polished and put-together, but relaxed and comfortable. More elevated than everyday casual, but not formal.

Tops:
Blouses, silk or cotton shirts, knit tops, tailored tees, cashmere sweaters
Bottoms:
Dark jeans (no distressing), chinos, tailored trousers, midi skirts, knee-length dresses
Footwear:
Ballet flats, loafers, low heels, ankle boots, clean leather sneakers
Outerwear:
Blazers, cardigans, structured jackets
Accessories:
Simple jewelry, leather handbag, scarves
For India Specifically:
  • ‚Ä¢ Longer hemlines (knee-length or below) for cultural respect
  • ‚Ä¢ Breathable fabrics: cotton, linen, or light silk
  • ‚Ä¢ Layering pieces for air-conditioned spaces
  • ‚Ä¢ Comfortable but stylish flats for walking
  • ‚Ä¢ Nice pashmina or scarf (style + temple coverage)

üëî Smart Casual for Men

Neat and intentional, stepping up from jeans-and-t-shirt, but not requiring a tie or suit.

Tops:
Collared shirts (button-down, polo), henley shirts, casual blazers, sweaters
Bottoms:
Chinos, dark jeans (well-fitted, no rips), dress pants (optional)
Footwear:
Leather loafers, oxford shoes, desert boots, clean minimalist sneakers
Outerwear:
Sport coat, blazer, bomber jacket
Accessories:
Leather belt, watch, minimal accessories
For India Specifically:
  • ‚Ä¢ Lightweight cotton or linen shirts
  • ‚Ä¢ Breathable chinos or cotton trousers
  • ‚Ä¢ Closed-toe shoes for temple visits
  • ‚Ä¢ Light layers for varying temperatures
  • ‚Ä¢ Avoid shorts at upscale venues

Quick Comparison:

Women: More flexibility with dresses, skirts, and varied silhouettes. Emphasis on accessories and layering options.
Men: Typically stick to shirt-and-pants formula. Focus on fit and quality of basics.

Context matters: A nice restaurant in Mumbai might expect dressier smart casual than a boutique hotel lunch. When in doubt in India, err on the side of slightly more modest and covered.
